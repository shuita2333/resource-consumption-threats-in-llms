# Attack

This page collects papers on resource-oriented attacks against large models, reasoning systems, and agentic workflows.

## Included Scope

- Resource exhaustion attacks
- Cost amplification attacks
- Inference inflation and reasoning overload
- Tool- or workflow-induced resource abuse
